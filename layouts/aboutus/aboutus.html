About Us

BioDati is empowering Life Sciences. With the largest computable biological knowledgebase, our mission is to advance research, drug development, precision medicine and data science/deep learning by making biological knowledge Shareable, Reusable and Computable.

BioDati allows all of your new research to easily be combined with your prior knowledge to be used by biologists and researchers to search, generate models, and analyze while maintaining full provenance of the knowledge at all times.
